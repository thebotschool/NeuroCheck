# NeuroCheck — Digital Assessment of a Schoolchild’s Learning Functions (7–9 Years)

We’ve gathered everything to make learning easy, joyful, and effective—at home and at school. Let’s unlock your child’s superpowers! 💪

## 1. Brief Report on Each Test

🔍 **Working Memory Test (CPT):** Your child tries to hold sequences in memory, but sometimes attention slips, especially in long or complex tasks. It’s like a battery that drains quickly—but we know how to recharge it! 🔋

🚦 **Self-Control Test (Go/No-Go):** Your child is a whirlwind of energy! They act quickly, but sometimes impulses take over, making it hard to wait their turn or listen fully. It’s like a puppy learning to wait for a command before jumping! 🐶

👀 **Visual Attention Test (TCP):** Here’s where your child shines brighter than the stars! 🌟 In clear, structured tasks with straightforward rules, they show super-focus. When everything is clear and distraction-free, they’re a champion of attention!

## 2. Guide to Your Child’s Success

This is your “magic guide” to your child—a map to unlock their potential and make learning an adventure! 📘

📌 **Individual Characteristics:**

- **Pace:** Fast and impulsive, like a meteor! For success, they need a calm rhythm with pauses to think things through. ⏳
- **Perception Style:** They grasp information best visually—bright charts, pictures, colorful lists. Long verbal instructions “fly away,” but visuals are their magic! 🎨
- **Attention Focus:** Like a battery: fully charged for short, engaging tasks but drains quickly in monotonous ones. In their favorite activities, they’re at maximum power! ⚡

🔎 **What Helps:**

- **Visual Supports:** Cards with plans, step-by-step diagrams, reminder stickers—these are like a treasure map! 🗺️
- **Step-by-Step Approach:** Break big tasks into small steps: not “do everything,” but “1. Read. 2. Underline. 3. Check.” Each win is a reason to celebrate! 🏆
- **“Stop” Rule:** Pause before acting: a deep breath or counting to three. It’s like a “pause” button in a game—helps them regroup! 🛑
- **Rhythm:** Use a timer for short sprints, tap out a rhythm—and attention dances in sync! 🎶

🚫 **What Hinders:**

- **Multitasking:** “Listen, remember, write” is like juggling balls—everything falls. One task at a time, and success is in the bag! ⚠️
- **Rushing and Switching:** Hurrying or sudden changes disrupt their rhythm. Calmness is the key to harmony! 🐢
- **Noise and Clutter:** Extra items, noise, or chatter are like radio static. A clean desk and quiet space keep focus at its peak! 🤫

## 3. Recommendations

Let’s create the perfect environment for your child—with warmth and care! ❤️

🏠 **At Home:**

- **Homework Organization:** Short sessions: 15–20 min of work + 5–7 min active breaks (jumping, drinking water). A visual timer is like a magic clock! 🕒 Create a 2–3 point plan and keep it visible. Desk only for work—no extras!

- **Support and Encouragement:** Praise effort: “You focused so well!” or “Great job waiting!” If they drift off, don’t scold—help: “Let’s figure it out together!” 🌈

- **Communication:** Eye-to-eye, slow, and have them repeat tasks in their own words. It’s a bridge to understanding! 👀

🏫 **At School:**

- **Task Instructions:** Clear, short, step-by-step—plus written on the board or a card. Sit up front, away from windows and doors, to avoid distractions. 📝

- **Lesson Structure:** Algorithm cards for routine tasks, pauses before questions. Dynamic but predictable pacing—like a favorite story! 📖

- **Individual Learning Plan (ILP):** If challenges persist, involve a school psychologist to build self-regulation. It’s like a personal brain coach! 🧠

## 4. Recommended Activities

Fun games that build skills unnoticed—like adventures in the land of knowledge! 🎉

- **For Attention:** “Proofreading Test”: Cross out all “A”s in a magazine in 2 min—letter hunt! 🕵️‍♂️ “Spot 10 Differences”: A detective puzzle for the eyes. Mazes: Trace with eyes first, then pencil—a path to treasure!

- **For Self-Control and Body Control:** “Freeze Game”: Freeze on signal—become a superhero statue! 🗽 “Edible/Non-Edible”: Catch the ball only for “edible” items—reaction + restraint! ⚽ “Don’t Say Yes or No”: Word control like a spy mission! 🕶️

- **For Memory:** “Memory Game”: Find matching cards—start with 4–6 pairs and grow! 🃏 “I Packed My Bag…”: Add items in a chain—memory chain! 🛍️

- **For Rhythm (Organizes the Brain):** “Echo”: Repeat a rhythm with claps—mountain echo! 🥁 Exercises to music: March or jump to the beat—brain dance! 🎵
